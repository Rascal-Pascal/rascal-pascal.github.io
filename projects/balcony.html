Barza

Reclaimed Stories

2017

     1800x750x400

     Lounge Sofa

     Teak · Leather · Fabric

Barza (Balcony) is a lounge sofa that accommodates the different habits of sitting in traditional India. Barza is composed of a simple, bold structure in solid teak, a long bolster backrest and a wide armrest overhang.

Reclaimed Stories is a project inspred by indian culture, developed and designed together with Nikita Bhate.
