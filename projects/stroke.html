tankan

Reclaimed Stories

2017

     1050x750x700

     Lounge chair

     Teak · Leather · Iron

Tankan (Stroke) is a lounge chair that expresses the idea of dualism. The stroke of the chair’s curve, reminds of the curvaceous lines of old, heavy iron outdoor furniture in India. It is bold, creating a strong presence; but this time for the interiors. The iron structure is hammered into shape by a local blacksmith - after which the hammer strokes on the structure gives a feeling of austerity.

Reclaimed Stories is a project inspired by indian culture, developed and designed together with Nikita Bhate.
