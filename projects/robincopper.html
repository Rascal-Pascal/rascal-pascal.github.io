Robin Copper

2013

     Lamp


     Copper · Wood · LED

An asymmetric profile is combined with freely attachable light clips. Robin Copper is totally flexible in its positioning. It sets light spots, but is not a lamp specific to only one use. Depending on the arrangement of the clips and the position of the profile, it is a desk lamp, floor lamp or pendant lamp and gives the user the opportunity to playfully reinvent and personalise light situations.
