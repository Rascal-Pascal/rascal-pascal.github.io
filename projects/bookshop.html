Bookshop

UdK Berlin

2011

     900x700x500

     book display


     wood · IKEA Antonius system ·

     screws

     Create a temporary exhibition space for over 500 books in 90 m2. The proposal is a reinterpretation of a typical wall mounted bookshelf system transformed into a modular table system.

     Presenting books horizontally provides the viewer the ability to open the book directly and browse through it. By organizing the tables in linear lines we achieve the accessibility from both sides and provoke communication among the visitors. With the use of standard components we reduced the price and formed an effective and simple temporary structure.
