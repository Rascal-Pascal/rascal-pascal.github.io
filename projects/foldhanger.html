Foldhanger

Airbnb

2015

plywood / metal wire hook / steel profile

Airbnb and Fabrica celebrate the connections that guests and hosts experience through sharing a home, and how these relationships begin with a simple welcome.

At Palazzo Crespi, a traditional villa in the heart of Milan, this connection was showcased by welcoming and guiding visitors through various exhibitions in a house. In the ballroom, we designed a series of living room furniture, inspired by the different traditions of seating coming from around the world (representing our multicultural backgrounds and the global accommodation of Airbnb).
