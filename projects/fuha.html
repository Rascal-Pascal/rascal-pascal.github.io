Fuha

Daikin

2015

     Installation


     Granite · Wood · Glass ·

     Ceramic

Under the art direction of Sam Baron and Studio Forma Fantasma, we created a multi-sensory platform where air is the designer.

The exhibition was named after two traditional Japanese onomatopoetic expressions reminiscent of the sound of human breath: FU and HA. FU HA reveals the various qualities air can assume, even presumably indistinct ones such as form and volume. An interactive printer shows the movement of air according to the user’s lung capacity. The weight of air is shown in an installation that uses various elements to show reciprocal tension.
