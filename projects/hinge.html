Hinge

Alcantara

2012

Chair

4 sheets of synthetic plates / alcantara / cushion

This is the outcome of a long process of bonding one synthetic material with another through heat.

We found a way to form a steady hinge between two materials by using only Alcantara® heat and pressure. Since none of the usual attachment materials are needed, this connection can be called “weightless”. The synthetic sheet is attached to its base by using a heated metallic stamp that melts the two materials together. The result is a very unique rivet-like look. Now we won’t have to recycle bonding material, we won’t have to pay for bonding material and we wont have to deal with the oldfashioned looks of bonding material.
