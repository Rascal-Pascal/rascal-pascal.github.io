Caraffa Collection

Fabrica x FRISUR

2016

     75x90x350

     Caraffe · Vase


     Italian ceramic

For SS16 FRISUR, a multidisciplinary design studio and fashion label based in Berlin, takes inspiration from interior design, namely the brand’s first ever design object: CASCADE, a minimal carafe with a cascading spout devised in collaboration by Fabrica’s German designer Pascal Hien. Entirely made in Italy and available in only 25 pieces for each of the three colors, the object inspires the collection and viceversa, closing the gap between concept and product.
