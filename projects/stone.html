Stone / Alcantara

This is a kickoff project in which we discovered how to cushion a seat with a completely new attachment technique.

Hard surfaces like stone, ceramic or concrete are difficult to be cushioned by using common attachment techniques such as screwing, riveting or glueing. By using a blowtorch or heatgun we heat up a metallic stamp in order to partially melt Alcantara® onto a stone through pressure. With this technique we achieved a durable cushioning and connection between Alcantara® and the stone. No additive connecting material is included. The Alcantara® synthetic sheet is permanently melted onto the stone’s surface. For us this technique had a huge potential and lead to further interpretations. Our starting point to cushion the world!
