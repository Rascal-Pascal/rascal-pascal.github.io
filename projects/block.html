Reclaimed Stories

2017

     450x450x700

     Dining chair

     Teak · Metal · Stone

Decoration is an integral part of India. Sancha is a block of wood intricately hand carved with floral patterns commonly used for textile block printing. The dining chair is inspired from this form - simple, straight and bold using three solid material blocks - wood, stone, metal. The construction is modular, with joinery details in brass.

Reclaimed Stories is a project inspred by indian culture, developed and designed together with Nikita Bhate.
