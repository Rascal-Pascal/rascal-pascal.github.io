Housewarming

Airbnb

2015

     Installation


     Plywood · Textile · Metal

     Airbnb and Fabrica celebrate the connections that guests and hosts experience through sharing a home, and how these relationships begin with a simple welcome.
     Palazzo Crespi, a traditional villa in the heart of Milan, hosted the exhibition. Visitors were welcomed and guided through various rooms with personal installations. A series of living room furniture was designed for the ballroom, inspired by the different traditions of seating coming from around the world. As project leader, I guided the team to form a uniform collection that allowed guests to playfully discover the story behind each object, and relax.
