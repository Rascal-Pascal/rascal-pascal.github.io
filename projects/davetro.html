Da Vetro

Please Do Not Enter

2015

     Vase · Carafe · Bowl


     Glass

Da Vetro is a glass collection designed for Please Do Not Enter, a one-of-a-kind curated store in the heart of downtown Los Angeles. Entirely made by hand in Italy by the well-known glass blower Massimo Lunardon, each individual piece is numbered and produced in a limited edition of 30.
