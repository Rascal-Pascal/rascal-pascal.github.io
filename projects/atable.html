A Table Collection

Atipico

2015

     Tableware collection


     Ceramic · Wood · Steel

A tableware collection made in collaboration with Fabrica designers, founded on the diverse cultural backgrounds and eating traditions.

The present-day table is a melting pot of different cultures, customs and traditions. Increasingly, the contemporary palate seeks multi-ethnic culinary experiences and the dining table becomes a physical space for these exchanges. This is the driving concept behind ‘A Table’, a new collection of objects and accessories for the modern table – plates, bowls, glasses, baskets, carafes, cutting boards, cutlery and linen – designed by Fabrica for Atipico.
