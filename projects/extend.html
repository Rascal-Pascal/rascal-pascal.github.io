2017

     Deck chair

     Teak · Leather

The Ayāma (Extend) deck chair represents Indian lounge chairs which are often very low and comfortably wide. As a unique detail, the armrest is far extended or can be folded out to lift your feet for comfort. The construction is combining traditional reclaimed wood with lathe turning techniques.

Reclaimed Stories is a project inspired by indian culture, developed and designed together with Nikita Bhate.
