Steelcase Series 2 features the signature Air LiveBack® technology, which works through an innovative geometric design that provides sophisticated support. The specially engineered wave pattern is scaled to provide support for each part of the spine, allowing it to flex in multiple dimensions to create responsive, personalized comfort and fit.

The chair follows the original backrest design and patented technology of Amia Air. With Series 2 the Steelcase development team has turned it into a fully scalable taskchair platform.
