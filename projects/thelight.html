The second collaboration with the fashion brand FRISUR. In FW16 we take inspiration from finding warmth in the furthest reaches of the North. The soft light and tenderness of the oil lamp stands as a symbol for this collection. The feeling and ambience created is represented within the design of clothing, choice of materials and garments. The fluid and human like shape is always unique. Produced and hand blown in italy.
