Pascal Hien is a designer from Germany. After studio experiences at Arik Levy in Paris and a residency at Fabrica research center in Italy, he is currently working for Steelcase Inc. He collaborates as a creative consultant with brands amongst product, interior and fashion design.

His recent activity has led him towards user orientated and systemic industrial design while keeping close connection to artisanal crafts and limited editions. Driven by a curious and optimistic design method, his work wanders between function, elegance and irritation.
